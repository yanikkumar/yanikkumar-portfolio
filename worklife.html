Reach Me Out

Even though my profesional life started after college. My journey started during my good old school days. That Chapter-16 Understanding HTML of computer science in 8th Standard in the school attracted me towards the world wide web. I still remember that index.html highlighting my name Yanik Kumar inside a <marquee> with bright yellow backgound made me like I painted my name in web. With the early access to internet I uses to surf web with the curiousity looking through different websites, The vibrant colors and glowing text attracted me.
Back in those days during 11th-12th standard I use to learn do magic for fun and came accross few online stores selling magic items. Got more addicted to web and ecommerce and was curious to know how things are sold made. Learnt some tools to make videos and learnt photoshop as well to create images. During my college days expanded my skills to know more about ecommerce. Setup my first goDaddy Store with pure HTML CSS design to sell something. Soon I realised it takes alot to create an ecommerce platform. Learnt more skills that got me here to this path.

In my college days, I attended workshops related to web-desing and development and started to learn full stack development on my own. I wanted to completely understand the concept how the actual web platfomrs work and how this online world work behind the scenes. My online presence started back in 2011 with my first gmail account and facebook account when it was uprising, and this online presence gave me so much which helped me understand the online world.
I did trained myself with Digital Marketing Platforms in 2016 and started building something on my own. While learning full stack development I did training from MCN Solutions Pvt. Ltd. in Complete Web Development and gained so much knowledge that time. Also worked with PHPTPOINT with some content writing and some small projects. Build few projects on my own and started working on my personal close to hear project Believe Master. That training during college time helped me gain so much web knowledge about web and landed my first Internship as a Frontend Developer during my final semester.
Kingster636 Technologies Pvt. Ltd. My first compnay that taught me the reality of the startup world, the term "Entrepreneurship" Helped me start my working world. Gained so much experience by putting alot of efforts (the failed but learning efforts) which taught me what NOT TO DO before building or starting anything.
